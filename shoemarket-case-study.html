The Shoe Market

The Shoe Market is a family-owned footwear store dedicated to providing high-quality, comfort-focused shoes. The store curates a thoughtful selection of European and U.S. brands known for superior comfort, durability, and orthopedic support. Whether shopping for everyday shoes, orthopedic footwear, or seasonal selections, The Shoe Market aims to create a welcoming, informative experience—both in-store and online.

Project Overview

Role: UX Researcher and Designer

Goal: My goal for this project is to redesign The Shoe Market's website to improve content structure, accessibility, and user experience for its target audience.

Approach: Bottom-Up — Preserve Target's iconic aesthetic as much as possible, while implementing thoughtful adjustments to enhance navigation and user engagement.

DISCOVER & DEFINE

Exploratory User Research

Research Goals

  • Understand the needs and pain points of The Shoe Market's primary customer base.
  • Evaluate the current website's usability and content organization.
  • Explore ways to better connect the digital experience to the in-store experience.

Intended Research Methods

  • Informal interviews with in-store customers and staff.
  • Preliminary competitor review.
  • Conduct a short customer survey (both in sotre and online) to gather feedback on browsing habits, shopping behaviors, and what customers expect or want to see more of.
