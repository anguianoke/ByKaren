Art Board

Drawing allows me to express myself in a way that feels unrestricted. At times, I forget how much satisfaction comes from the simple act of illustrating the things that I find most endearing. These pieces are the result of quiet evenings spent capturing those moments. I love to create for myself, using drawing as a form of journaling, and I share them here to remind us of the little things that bring us fulfillment.
